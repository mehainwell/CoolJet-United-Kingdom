<h4>How Does CoolJet Portable AC Work?</h4>
<p>CoolJet Portable AC generally operates using <strong>evaporative cooling technology</strong>, which cools air naturally through water evaporation.</p>
<p>The process is straightforward:</p>
<ol>
<li>
<p>Fill the built-in water tank with clean water.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>The internal filter absorbs the water.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>A fan draws warm air through the damp cooling filter.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>As the water evaporates, it absorbs heat from the incoming air.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>The device releases cooler, more refreshing air into the surrounding area.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<p>Some models also allow users to add ice cubes to the water tank for an enhanced cooling effect. This method is especially effective in dry climates where evaporation occurs more efficiently.</p>
